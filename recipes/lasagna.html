Sicilian Lasagna

Born in Naples, perfected in Sicily

Lasagna is an italian dish composed of pasta sheets generally layered with some kind of meat, cheese, and sauce.

This particular recipe calls for a creamy bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Salt
  • Ragu
  • Flour
  • Ground Beef

Instructions

  1. Fry the ground beef with onions and SPG until brown.
  2. Combine equal parts butter and flour in a saucepan and stir until the flour is cooked.
  3. Boil the lasagna with heavily salted water until they're half-cooked.
  4. Create the layers repeating in this order: sheet, meat, sheet, ragu, sheet, bechamel.
