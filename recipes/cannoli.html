Sicilian Cannoli

A favourite of Queen Elizabeth

Cannoli is an Italian dessert made with a deep-fried pastry shell containing a sweet ricotta filling.

Get creative with the toppings for this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Ricotta
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Pistachio

Instructions

  1. Roll the dough out to a thickness of about 1 inch.
  2. Form the cannoli shape, and drop it in a pot of oil heated to about 354 Celsius.
  3. Mix a cup of ricotta cheese with 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 egg yolks.
  4. Leave the fried pastry shells to rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Using a piping bag, fill the pastry shells with the ricotta mix.
  6. Now throw it out and go make yourself some oatmeal.
